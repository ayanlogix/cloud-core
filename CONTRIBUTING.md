1.  **Scaling Logic**: Improving the heuristic engine for auto-scaling simulations.
2.  **Telemetry Precision**: Enhancing the real-time charting and network monitoring throughput.
3.  **UI/UX Modularization**: Keeping the grid visualization decoupled and easy to extend.
